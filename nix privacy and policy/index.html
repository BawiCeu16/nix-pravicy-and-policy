privacy policy

privacy first

nix is an open-source application. it does not collect, store, or share any personal information. we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.

everything runs locally on your device. your data always stays with you.

how the app works

nix is designed to work entirely offline and relies only on the features of your device. the app requests minimal permissions required for its core functionality (such as accessing files if you choose to open them). these permissions are used solely for their intended purpose and never for tracking or profiling.

no information is sent to external servers. the app functions independently on your device and does not rely on cloud services.

because the project is open-source, anyone can review the code to verify that it operates as described.

third-party sources
